= 2180. Count Integers With Even Digit Sum

https://leetcode.com/problems/count-integers-with-even-digit-sum/[LeetCode - 2180. Count Integers With Even Digit Sum ^]

Given a positive integer `num`, return _the number of positive integers *less than or equal to*_ `num` _whose digit sums are *even*_.

The *digit sum* of a positive integer is the sum of all its digits.

 
<strong class="example">Example 1:*

[subs="verbatim,quotes"]
----
*Input:* num = 4
*Output:* 2
*Explanation:*
The only integers less than or equal to 4 whose digit sums are even are 2 and 4.    
----

<strong class="example">Example 2:*

[subs="verbatim,quotes"]
----
*Input:* num = 30
*Output:* 14
*Explanation:*
The 14 integers less than or equal to 30 whose digit sums are even are
2, 4, 6, 8, 11, 13, 15, 17, 19, 20, 22, 24, 26, and 28.
----

 
*Constraints:*


* `1 <= num <= 1000`
